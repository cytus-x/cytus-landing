Why Cytus?

High Capital Utilization Rate

With WUSDY, users can receive interest (via rebasing) and use wUSDY as collateral to borrow at the same time, i.e. lever up. This provides huge improvement of capital efficiency compared to other protocols.
‍

Deep Liquidity

USDY holders enjoy deep liquidity via swapping USDY for other coins at any time. To users, providing liquidity via stables (USDY) do not suffer from impermanent loss, which boosts liquidity provision
‍


‍

No lock Up Period
‍

For most RWA protocols, once users invest, they cannot exit their position for a period of time because it's locked in RWA. In Cytus, users can exit anytime by selling USDY on the open market.
‍

Diversified Treasury
‍

USDY holders are exposed to a basket of highquality and well-diversified RWA in the treasury. Diversification is the only free lunch in investing.
